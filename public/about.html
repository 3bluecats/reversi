A Little About This Game

Let's Play

Hi! My name is Caryn and I am a graduate student at UCI. This is my first ever web programmed game. This old Engish game is called Reversi. It was invented in 1886. In order to win, you have to be strategic and mindful of the other player's moves. In this version, instead of being assigned a traditional black or white coin, each player is assigned a black or white flower. Enjoy!
